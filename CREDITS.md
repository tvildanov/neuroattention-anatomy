Z-Anatomy is itself based on the BodyParts3D / Anatomography model
(© DBCLS, CC-BY-SA 2.1 JP).

## Modifications
- Per-system FBX exports converted to glTF (GLB) with Draco mesh compression
  via Blender headless.
- Normalized to a shared coordinate frame (centered, height ≈ 2.0 units) for
  the NeuroAttention BodyAtlas tool.
- Materials stripped (the atlas applies its own x-ray shader at runtime).

As required by ShareAlike, these derived models remain under CC-BY-SA 4.0.
